Illustrating the Gender Gap in Fiction

Andy

2019-10-10

Loganberry Books, an independent bookstore in Cleveland, intitiated a social experiment for in March 2019 called Illustrating the Gender Gap in Fiction. This experiment, in honor of Womens History Month, was decribed as, a live performance art project where we will shelve the works by men in our LitArts room backwards. What a cool idea, and very eye-opening.

This would likely be worse if the experiment was carried out with mathematics, statistics, and computer science books. May have to replicate this in my office at some point.
